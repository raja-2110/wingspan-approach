Air Traffic Controller

A career that elevates

Overview

Air Traffic Controllers (ATCs) ensure the safe and orderly movement of aircraft in the air and on the ground. They manage takeoffs, landings, and flight paths to prevent collisions and minimize delays.

Eligibility

  • Graduate in Science/Engineering or B.Sc. with Physics & Mathematics
  • Must pass AAI ATC Entrance Exam
  • Good communication & multitasking skills
  • Medical fitness as per AAI guidelines

Training Path

ATCs are trained at the Civil Aviation Training College (CATC) after selection. Training includes simulations, radar operations, and real-world tower control.

Career Opportunities

Work with Airports Authority of India (AAI), private ATC consultancies, and international aviation hubs.

Expected Salary

Starting salary is ₹40,000–₹70,000 per month, increasing with experience and roles such as Senior Controller or Tower In-Charge.

Ground Staff

A career that supports aviation from the ground

Overview

Ground Staff play a crucial role in airport operations, handling passenger services, baggage, and aircraft turnaround. They ensure smooth functioning on the ground so flights can take off safely and on time.

Eligibility

  • 10+2 or any undergraduate degree
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills
  • Basic computer knowledge
  • Medical fitness and grooming standards

Training Path

Ground staff usually undergo short-term diploma or certificate courses in aviation, hospitality, or ground handling from recognized institutes.

Career Opportunities

Work with airlines, airport authorities, ground handling agencies, and cargo operators in roles like check-in, boarding gate, baggage services, and ramp assistance.

Expected Salary

Starting salaries range from ₹20,000–₹35,000/month, with scope for promotion to supervisor or terminal manager roles.

Commercial Pilot

Explore your journey to the skies

Overview

A Commercial Pilot flies aircraft for compensation or hire. From flying domestic airlines to international jets, this is one of the most prestigious and rewarding careers in aviation.

Eligibility

  • Must be at least 18 years old
  • Passed 10+2 with Physics & Mathematics(50% is required)
  • Class 1 Medical Certificate
  • Fluent in English

Training Path

After obtaining a Student Pilot License (SPL), you’ll move on to a Private Pilot License (PPL), and then complete 200 flying hours for your Commercial Pilot License (CPL).

Career Opportunities

Work with top airlines such as IndiGo, Air India, Emirates, or go into charter aviation, cargo, or even corporate flying.

Expected Salary

Starting salary ranges between ₹1.5–2.5 lakhs/month in India. With international experience or command upgrades, earnings can rise substantially.

Cabin Crew (Flight Attendant)

A career that elevates

Overview

Cabin Crew ensure passenger safety and comfort during flights. They represent the airline's brand and handle emergencies, service, and communication.

Eligibility

  • Minimum 10+2 education
  • Good communication & personality
  • Height & medical requirements (as per airline)
  • Fluency in English & other languages preferred

Training Path

Airlines provide in-house training or accept candidates from certified aviation academies. Training covers emergency procedures, service, and grooming.

Career Opportunities

Opportunities with domestic and international airlines. Growth into Senior Crew, Purser, or ground trainer roles.

Expected Salary

₹25,000–₹80,000/month for freshers; international airlines pay more with additional perks.

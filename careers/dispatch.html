Flight Dispatcher

A career that elevates

Overview

Flight Dispatchers are ground-based aviation professionals who assist in flight planning, monitor weather, fuel loads, and ensure safe and efficient operations in coordination with the pilot.

Eligibility

  • 10+2 or graduate with strong aviation knowledge
  • Must complete DGCA-approved Flight Dispatcher course
  • Excellent communication and decision-making skills
  • Strong understanding of meteorology and flight operations

Training Path

Typically includes 6–12 months of classroom training and simulations. Includes modules on navigation, aircraft systems, and flight planning.

Career Opportunities

Jobs with airlines, airport operations, cargo carriers, and international aviation firms.

Expected Salary

₹40,000–₹90,000/month; senior dispatchers or international operators earn even more.

About Us

Wingspan Approach is a digital platform crafted for aspiring aviators, flight enthusiasts, and aviation professionals. We aim to bridge the gap between pilot training and modern technology by offering practical tools like mock test generators, digital logbooks, CPL cost estimators, and more.

Our Vision

To empower the next generation of pilots and aviation lovers with smart, accessible, and interactive learning and record-keeping tools.

Why Choose Us?

  • Cloud sync options to access your data anywhere
  • Realistic mock test simulations for DGCA & CPL exams
  • Professional digital pilot logbook & hours tracking
  • Support for aspiring pilots around the world
